Cookies are small data files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website or use an app. Cookies are widely used to make websites and apps work, or work more efficiently, as well as to provide reporting information and personalize your experience. We use both first-party cookies (set by Nazdeeki) and third-party cookies (set by others, such as analytics or advertising partners). We may also use web beacons (tracking pixels) and similar technologies for similar purposes.

We use cookies for the following reasons: Essential Cookies: Required for the technical operation of our Platforms and to enable core features (e.g., account login, order placement). Performance and Functional Cookies: Enhance the performance and functionality of our Platforms (e.g., remembering user preferences, enabling chat support). Analytics Cookies: Collect information about how users interact with our Platforms to help us improve them and understand usage trends. Advertising Cookies: Make advertising messages more relevant to you and measure the effectiveness of our campaigns.
